Contact

Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?

On your device, and — only if you chose Sync With iCloud — in your own private iCloud account. The developer has no access to it. See the privacy policy for details.

How do I turn iCloud sync on or off?

Go to Profile → Data Sync. You can start syncing to iCloud, or remove the iCloud copy and keep everything on this device only.

Which CSV files can I import?

Export files from Anycoin, Delta, and Binance. Only Bitcoin activity is imported — rows for other assets are skipped and listed in the import preview.

Do recurring buys purchase Bitcoin for me?

No. TrackYourSats never places trades, holds funds, or connects to your exchange account. Recurring buy rules only record tracker-side entries for purchases you make yourself, so your DCA history stays effortless to maintain.

Which currencies are supported?

Portfolio values can be shown in USD, EUR, or CZK. The live Bitcoin price comes from Coinbase (BTC-USD) and is converted using current exchange rates.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual transactions and portfolios in the app, reset the device's data from Profile → Data Sync, or delete the app. If iCloud sync was on, Remove iCloud Data deletes the synced copy as well.

Privacy

TrackYourSats requires no account and collects no personal information. Read the full privacy policy.
